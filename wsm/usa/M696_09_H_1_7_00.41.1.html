Refitting the handlebar
Position and orientate the handlebar (4) so that the punch marks (A) are equidistant from the lower clamp and aligned with the face of the clamp.
Apply the recommended grease to the threads and undersides of the heads of screws (3).
 Important
Position the clamps with the arrows (B) pointing towards the fuel tank.
 
Refit the upper clamp (1) and insert the screws (3).
Tighten the screws (3) to the specified torque (Sect. C 3, Frame torque settings) in the sequence 1-2-3-4-1-2, as shown in the figure.
